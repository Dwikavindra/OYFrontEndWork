Types of Integration for OY! Multitransfer

via Integrasi (API Multitransfer)

With simple and fast API integration, OY! Multitransfer is directly connected to various banks and e-Wallet. Send funds directly through your system or platform with Callback URL option for easier integration with OY!

via Dashboard (Bulk Multitransfer)

Without integration, you can directly make transfers via the dashboard. Simply upload a .CSV file or fill in the transaction information on OY! Bisnis dashboard.

via Routing Solution

With one simple integration (through a routing system), you get to access payment links and disbursement products. Whenever you receive funds, the routing system will allow you to send the funds automatically up to 10 recipient accounts.

Financial Company - Investment

With OY! Multitransfer, investment companies will experience convenience in transferring funds to user and to the custodian bank (e.g. Bibit).

Financial Company - Credit / P2P Lending

OY! Multitransfer will help distribute loan funds to borrowers from credit/P2P lending companies (e.g. Pegadaian, Investree, Koinworks).

Payment Point Online Banking (PPOB)

Penyedia jasa PPOB dapat menggunakan OY! Multitransfer untuk mengirim dana ke tujuan yang diminta dan melakukan top-up saldo e-Wallet.

eCommerce / Marketplace

E-commerce platforms or marketplaces can efficiently send funds to merchants/partners/agents and send sales report to distributors/vendors. In addition, distribution of prizes, cashback, cash, and e-Wallet balances can also be sent via OY! Multitransfer.

FMCG / Retail / Logistik (Tokopedia, gudangada, ids, orderkuota)

FMGC / Retail / Logistics companies can use OY! Multitransfer to send funds to distributors easily (example: Tokopedia, Gudangada, IDS, Orderkuota).

Online Shop

Online shop business owners can use OY! Multitransfer to make payments to suppliers or vendors and distribute gifts in the form of balances (cash) to customers.
