Variation of Features on OY! Payment Link

Set Link Active Duration (Reusable or One-Time)

Choose the type of Payment Link you need: One-Time for one-time use or Reusable for repeated use (required to repeat). Reusable type can be used for various transactions without an expiration time, while One-Time has one.

Set Payment Amount (Open Amount or Closed Amount)

Select the type of payment amount you want: Open Amount (flexible payment amount, customers need to fill in the payment amount themselves) or Closed Amount (payment amount is set by you so customers can make payments directly)

Suit Payment Page Interface According to Business Branding

To maintain your business branding, you can customize the payment link page interface through the dashboard. Click for more info. (https://docs.oyindonesia.com/#pop-seamless-payment-experience-payment-links-invoice)

Send Payment Link via Email and/or WhatsApp

You will get a notification containing a payment link for your customers as a reminder to make a payment. Build customer trust with our services.

Types of Integration for OY! Payment Link

via Desktop or Mobile Dashboard

Without integration, you still can enjoy OY! Payment Link for your business. Choose the desired platform, from dashboard access via a laptop/PC to via smartphones for easy access.

via Integrasi

OY! Payment Link is available via API Integration. With a quick and easy process, you can automatically create payment links through your platform whenever a customer wants to make a payment.

via Routing Solution

With one simple integration (through a routing system), you will get OY! Payment link and OY! Multitransfer all at once. Whenever you receive funds, the routing system will allow you to send the funds automatically up to 10 recipient accounts.
